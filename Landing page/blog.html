Gaganyaan

The Gaganyaan project is one of ISRO's latest projects. The project aims to demonstrate India's human spaceflight capabilities by launching a three-person crew into a 400 km orbit for a three-day mission.

Aditya-L1

Aditya-L1 is a spacecraft designed to study the Sun's atmosphere. It was launched on September 2, 2023, and reached its target orbit on January 6, 2024.Aditya-L1 is the first Indian space-based observatory to study the Sun.
